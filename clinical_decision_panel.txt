
Clinical Decision Panel

Why Alzheimer’s is serious:
Alzheimer’s disease creates progressive cognitive decline, functional dependency, caregiver burden, and long-term health system pressure.

Why trials fail:
The disease has multiple interacting mechanisms, long progression, late diagnosis, weak translation from animal models, and difficulty proving meaningful cognitive benefit.

Highest pathway failure signal in this dataset:
Lifestyle / Digital

Most promising repeated intervention signal:
donepezil

Clinician action:
Screen memory complaints early, review medications, manage vascular/metabolic risk, support caregivers, refer to memory clinics, and identify eligible patients for clinical trials.

Research action:
Focus on earlier disease stages, biomarker-positive populations, combination therapies, better cognitive endpoints, longer follow-up, and diverse populations.

AI/data analytics action:
Use analytics for trial matching, patient stratification, recruitment feasibility, site selection, biomarker intelligence, safety monitoring, outcome prediction, and repurposing prioritization.
