 AI-Driven Alzheimer's Clinical Intelligence Dashboard

An end-to-end healthcare analytics project that transforms publicly available Alzheimer's clinical trial data into actionable research intelligence using *Python*, *Streamlit*, and *Power BI*.

This project demonstrates how clinical trial data can be converted into interactive dashboards and decision-support analytics to assist researchers, healthcare analysts, and clinical trial teams in understanding the global Alzheimer's clinical research landscape.

 Project Overview

Alzheimer's disease remains one of the world's greatest healthcare challenges, with thousands of clinical trials investigating new therapeutic approaches. However, the large volume of publicly available clinical trial information makes it difficult to identify meaningful research patterns.

This project integrates healthcare analytics, data visualization, and clinical intelligence to provide an interactive platform for exploring Alzheimer's clinical trials, drug repurposing opportunities, biological pathways, sponsor performance, and research trends.

 Project Objectives

- Transform large-scale clinical trial data into meaningful healthcare insights.
- Analyze the global Alzheimer's clinical trial landscape.
- Identify promising drug repurposing opportunities.
- Evaluate biological pathway targeting.
- Explore sponsor performance and regional trial representation.
- Demonstrate healthcare analytics using Power BI and Python.
- Showcase an interactive clinical intelligence platform for research applications.
